Mesh

A$AP Mob is comprised of notorious hip-hop artists such as A$AP Rocky and A$AP Ferg. A$AP TyY hopes to be the newest member of the group's long list of successful artists. His first mixtape with the mob, "Best Kept Secret," received a large amount of hype before its release on April 13, 2016. The track list consists of multiple urban elements, customary of A$AP works, that gives the rapper an edgy feel that stays true to where he came from.
